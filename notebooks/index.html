While molecules are 3D objects and are typically drawn as 2.5D graphs (chirality is the 0.5), 2D string representations are useful in cheminformatics. Common methods for representing molecules as strings include SMILES and InChI. Other useful conventions to study include SMARTS for substructures and SMIRKS for reactions.
